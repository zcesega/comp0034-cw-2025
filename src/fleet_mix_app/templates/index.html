Welcome

The Fleet Mix Shift Inspector is a data-driven web application that quantifies how Singapore's car fleet composition changes between two years, using CC bands and manufacturer data.

Use the Fleet mix comparison view to compare the distribution of vehicles across CC bands between two years and to view the Mix Shift Score and top contributing bands. Use the Manufacturers view to explore the top manufacturers in a selected year.
